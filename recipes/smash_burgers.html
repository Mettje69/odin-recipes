Smash Burgers

Description

This is a classic smash burger recipe. It's a delicious and easy-to-make dish that's perfect for any occasion.

Ingredients

  • 1 lb ground beef (80% lean)
  • 4 hamburger buns
  • 4 slices of cheddar cheese
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • Optional toppings: lettuce, tomato, pickles, onions, ketchup, mustard

Steps

  1. Preheat a cast-iron skillet or griddle over medium-high heat.
  2. Divide the ground beef into 4 equal portions and shape them into loose balls.
  3. Place the beef balls on the hot skillet and smash them down with a spatula until they are about 1/2 inch thick.
  4. Season the patties with salt and pepper.
  5. Cook for 2-3 minutes until the edges are crispy, then flip the patties and cook for another 1-2 minutes.
  6. Place a slice of cheddar cheese on each patty during the last minute of cooking to melt.
  7. Toast the hamburger buns on the skillet until golden brown.
  8. Assemble the burgers with your desired toppings and serve immediately.
