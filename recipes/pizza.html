Pizza

Description

This is a classic pepperoni pizza recipe. It's a delicious and easy-to-make dish that's perfect for any occasion.

Ingredients

  • 1 pizza dough
  • 1/2 cup pizza sauce
  • 1 1/2 cups shredded mozzarella cheese
  • 20 slices of pepperoni
  • 1 tablespoon olive oil
  • 1 teaspoon dried oregano

Steps

  1. Preheat the oven to 475°F (245°C).
  2. Roll out the pizza dough on a floured surface.
  3. Spread the pizza sauce evenly over the dough.
  4. Top with shredded mozzarella cheese and pepperoni slices.
  5. Bake for 12-15 minutes until the crust is golden and cheese is bubbly.
